Shift in Population Drivers

info

British Columbia is experiencing a significant shift in demographic trends. Natural growth is plateauing while international migration has surged to record levels, now accounting for over 90% of total population growth. This executive summary tracks the velocity of these changes and their direct implications for provincial infrastructure and labor supply forecasts.
